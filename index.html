Urban Heat Island Analysis: Baltimore, MD

This site presents an analysis of the current and projected Urban Heat Island (UHI) effect in Baltimore using observed data and CMIP6 climate projections.

Project Overview

Determine the current Baltimore Urban Heat Island (UHI) and use local weather data and CMIP6 data to estimate the impact of climate change on the Baltimore UHI in 2050.
